    House of Wickeds

    House of Wickeds is a striking architectural masterpiece located in the heart of Miami, blending modern sophistication with a hint of dark, avant-garde design. The structure boasts clean, angular lines and towering glass façades that reflect the vibrant city skyline. Its exterior combines sleek black steel and deep charcoal concrete, creating a bold contrast against Miami's tropical backdrop.
